This webpage was created to allow users to access multiple quizzes and gain knowledge on the game "Valorant". Valorant is a team based tactical first-person hero shooter made by Riot Games where you play with a team of 5 against 5 each playing unique agents/characters on a variety of maps with an array of weapons at your disposal. There is currently three seperate types of quizzes which each test your knowledge on a specific category. The three quiz topics consist of a quiz on the Maps in the game, the Agents that you can play as and the Weapons that are available to you.

For users unfamiliar with the game or for users who want a refresh there is a Practice section of the website where you can learn all about each specific category and find the answers for the possible quiz questions.
